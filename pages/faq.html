Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about shopping with ABC Books

How can I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you will receive an email with a tracking number and a link to the carrier's website. You can also view your order status in the "My Orders" section of your account.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), UPI payments (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), Wallets, and NetBanking through our secure payment partner, Razorpay.

Do you offer free shipping?

Yes! We offer free standard shipping on all orders nationwide across India, regardless of the order value.

Can I cancel my order?

You can cancel your order within 2 hours of placing it. Please contact our support team or use the cancellation button in your order history if available.

Are the books original copies?

Absolutely. Every book sold on ABC Books is 100% original and genuine. We source directly from publishers and authorized distributors.

How do I return a book?

If you're not satisfied with your purchase, you can initiate a return within 30 days of delivery. Please email us at abcpublishinghouse@gmail.com with your order number to start the process.

Do you ship internationally?

Currently, we exclusively ship within India. We are working on expanding our services to international readers soon.
