FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does delivery take?

Standard delivery takes 5-7 business days. Express delivery (available for select pin codes) takes 2-3 business days. You can track your order using the tracking ID sent to your email.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for most items. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, you can return it for a full refund. The book must be in original condition with all packaging intact.

Do you offer Cash on Delivery?

Yes! We offer Cash on Delivery (COD) across most pin codes in India. You can also pay using UPI, credit/debit cards, or net banking for a seamless checkout experience.

Are all books original?

Absolutely! We guarantee that all books sold on ABC Books are 100% genuine and original. We source directly from publishers and authorized distributors.

How can I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you'll receive an email with a tracking ID. You can use this ID to track your order on our website or the courier partner's website.

Can I cancel my order?

Yes, you can cancel your order before it's shipped. Once shipped, you'll need to wait for delivery and then initiate a return if needed. Contact our support team for assistance.
