How to Use ImgBB

Free & Easy Image Hosting for Your Book Covers

1

Go to ImgBB Website

Open https://imgbb.com in your browser.

No account required! You can upload images without signing up.

Open ImgBB
2

Upload Your Book Cover Image

On the ImgBB homepage, you'll see a large upload area. You have 3 options:

  • Drag & Drop: Drag your image file directly to the upload area
  • Click to Browse: Click "Start uploading" and select your image
  • Paste from Clipboard: If you copied an image, just paste it (Ctrl+V)
Supported formats: JPG, PNG, GIF, WEBP | Max size: 32 MB
3

Wait for Upload to Complete

The image will upload automatically. You'll see a progress bar.

Once complete, you'll be taken to the image page with sharing options.

4

Get the Direct Image Link

After upload, you'll see several link options. Look for "Direct link".

Click on the "Direct link" box - it will automatically copy to clipboard!

Example Direct Link:
https://i.ibb.co/abc123/book-cover.jpg
Important: Make sure to copy the "Direct link", NOT the "HTML" or "BBCode" links. The direct link should start with https://i.ibb.co/
5

Use the Link in ABC Books Admin

Now you can use this direct link in your ABC Books admin panel:

  1. Open your ABC Books admin panel
  2. Click "Add Book" or "Edit Book"
  3. In the "Book Cover Image" field, paste the ImgBB direct link
  4. The image preview will appear automatically
  5. Save your book!
Open Admin Panel

Pro Tips

  • Free Forever: ImgBB offers free image hosting with no expiration
  • No Login Required: Upload without creating an account (but creating one lets you manage uploads)
  • Fast CDN: Images are served from a fast Content Delivery Network
  • Resize Option: Before uploading, you can choose to auto-resize large images
  • Organize Better: Create a free account to organize your uploads in albums
  • Keep Track: Save the ImgBB page URL (not just direct link) to edit/delete images later
